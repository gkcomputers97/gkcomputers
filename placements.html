Placements

Method of Placement

Companies approach GK Computers frequently seeking for a student who is willing to work. We take the contact from them and approach students who is ready to attend the interview, either they have completed course or still perusing or at the end of the course, we select the candidates as per requirement and inform to attend the interview.

GK Computers is planning to start a complete full-time placement service for the students in near future.

Placement Guidance Class

GK Computers have designed a course on placement guidance which is free and embedded on almost all the courses. It’s a 3-hour course, once you complete this course you will get an idea about attending an interview with small or big companies. It covers:

  • Difference between Bio-Data, Resume an CV.
  • Preparing a proper Bio-Data, Resume or CV.
  • Dress code, hair style and smile.
  • Entering interviewers’ room, greet and sitting procedure.
  • Detail introduction of “Tell me about yourself”.
  • What is eye contact and facing interviewer?
  • How to listen the question and answer in short?.
  • How to take time to think without knowing the interviewer?
  • How to convert your “weaknesses to strength”?
  • How to thank before coming out of the interview room?
  • How to approach the company in case of no response from them?
  • Why companies not select you thou you are technical sound good?
  • 5 most important ethics to attend interview.
  • Answer to more questions like this will be covered.

Placement Fee

It’s a free service from us.

For the placement service we don’t charge any amount from student end or from companies end. It’s a free service we provide, as of now we don’t promise student that we give 100% placement. It depends on the company approach to us, if the company job profile matches yours, we will be selecting you and sending for interview. Thou you have completed your course with us we will keep in touch with you and inform you about the interviews happening.
